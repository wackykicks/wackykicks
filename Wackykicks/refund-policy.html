Return Policy - The Basics

Product Return Conditions

  • Items must be well packed and in unused, new condition.
  • Must be sent via DTDC or India Post.
  • Sender’s address is mandatory on the package.

Return Timeline

  • The parcel must be shipped within 24 hours after we provide the return address.
  • Once shipped, you must share a photo of the package, tracking ID, and update us within 7 days.
  • We are not responsible for parcels lost or missing after 7 days of dispatch.
