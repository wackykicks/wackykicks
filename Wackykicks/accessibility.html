Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

At WACKYKICKS, we are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive experience for all visitors and continuously work to improve the accessibility of our website.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML for screen reader compatibility
  • Alt text for all important images
  • High-contrast text and accessible font sizing
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Responsive design for all devices

Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously reviewing and improving our site to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. As our content and tools grow, we aim to ensure accessibility remains a top priority.

Third-Party Content
